Champ Build Finder v1.0.1

Find your champions build quickly and consistently on your favorite build recommendation website.

🚀 Quick Start Guide

This extension operates directly inside your address bar through a simple keyword:

1. Click your address bar (or press Ctrl+L)
2. Type build and press Space
3. Type a champion name (e.g., Aatrox or TF)
4. Press Enter to be directed to the champs build page!

Supported Websites

You can select your preferred build recommendation website in the extension's pooup menu. Supported websites include:

U.GG OP.GG Blitz Lolalytics Mobalytics Poro.gg METAsrc
