Information Collection and Use

Champ Build Finder is designed with a Privacy-First, Local-Only architecture. We prioritize user data sovereignty and transparency:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, search history, or user data to external servers.
  • Local Processing: All extension configurations are stored exclusively on your local device.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Because we do not collect your data, we do not (and cannot) share or sell your information to any third party.

Security

We value your trust. Since the extension operates entirely on your local machine, your data security is handled by your browser's native security protocols. We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting the integrity of the extension's code.

Children’s Privacy

This Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
